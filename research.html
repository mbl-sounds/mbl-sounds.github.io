Research Interests

I am a PhD researcher at STADIUS, ESAT, KU Leuven, Belgium, supervised by Toon van Waterschoot, Marc Moonen, Jan Østergaard, Jesper Jensen, and collaborating with Filip Elvander and Randall Ali.

Main Themes

  • Acoustic Signal Processing – Estimation algorithms for room acoustics.
  • Distributed Signal Processing – Acoustic signal processing in Wireless Acoustic Sensor Networks (WASNs).
  • Room Acoustic Modeling – Tensor representations of room acoustic impulse responses.

Current Focus

Currently, I am working on physics-informed communication strategies in wireless acoustic sensor networks.

  • Latent spaces in kernel and boundary methods
  • Rate-distortion theory in room acoustic environments
